Frequently Asked Questions

Which stores are supported?

We support hundreds of popular online retailers including Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Best Buy, Target, and many more. Our system automatically detects and tracks prices from most major e-commerce platforms.

How often are prices updated?

We check prices multiple times per day for all tracked products. Premium plans get even more frequent updates, with prices checked every few hours to ensure you never miss a deal.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no questions asked. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! All new users get a 14-day free trial with full access to Pro features. No credit card required to start your trial.
